How It Works

Three steps to AI-powered DJ sessions.

01

Connect Controller

Plug in your Pioneer DDJ-FLX4 via USB. RhythmClaw auto-detects the controller and maps all MIDI controls.

02

AI Analyzes

Python engine analyzes your music library — BPM detection, key analysis, energy mapping, and harmonic compatibility.

03

Seamless Mixing

AI handles beat-matching and crossfading while you focus on track selection and crowd energy. Control via Telegram.
